Q: What safety measures are in place, especially considering the current circumstances?
A:The safety and well-being of our students are our top priorities. In response to current circumstances, we have implemented comprehensive safety protocols in accordance with local health guidelines. These measures include enhanced sanitation procedures, reduced class sizes to allow for physical distancing, mandatory mask-wearing, and temperature checks upon entry. We are committed to providing a safe and hygienic environment for our students to learn and thrive.
