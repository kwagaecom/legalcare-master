Corporate & Commercial Law

Overview:

A & B Law Ventures Corporate & Commercial Law related services includes:

  • Clear understanding of the legal issues and concerns involved in the commercial and contractual business
  • End-to-end services to clients in all aspects of commercial law including Contracts, Negotiable Instruments, Sale of Goods, Partnership, Competition, Trade Laws and issues arising under various contractual arrangements.
  • Interests of their clients in relation to all kinds of matters pertaining to Companies Act, 2013 before the National Company Law Tribunal (NCLT), National Company Law Appellate Tribunal (NCLAT), Securities and Exchange Board of India (SEBI), SEBI Appellate Tribunal (SAT) and before various judicial courts including the Hon’ble Supreme Court of India
  • Service Agreements, Distribution Agreements, Franchisee Agreements, Agreement to Sell, Deed of Mortgage, Industrial and Commercial Rent Agreements, Confidentiality and Non-Disclosure Agreements, Escrow Agreements etc.
  • Shareholders’ Agreements, Share Purchase Agreements, Joint Ventures and ancillary agreements like Services Agreements, Technology Agreements, etc.
  • Business Transfer Agreements and Asset Purchase Agreements
  • Documentation relating to Real estate financing, conveyancing, leasing, mortgaging, land use, subdivision, small business matters, international transactions
  • Conducting Due Diligence and advising clients about the way forward and chalking out strategies for mitigating the risks arising out of any legal liability, indulging into negotiations and coordination with various governmental departments and other bodies
  • Completing the compliances under Foreign Exchange Management Act, 1999 (FEMA) with respect to inbound investment.
  • Assisting our clients to invest in India under Foreign Direct Investment route across a wide array of sectors.

How Can We Help !

We are specializing in corporate and commercial law play a crucial role in assisting individuals and businesses in various ways:

  1. Business Formation: A & B Law Ventures help individuals and entrepreneurs in establishing new businesses by advising on the most suitable legal structure, such as sole proprietorship, partnership, corporation, or limited liability company (LLC). We assist in drafting and filing the necessary documentation to ensure compliance with state laws.

  2. Contracts and Agreements: A & B Law Ventures draft, review, and negotiate contracts and agreements essential for business operations, such as partnership agreements, employment contracts, non-disclosure agreements (NDAs), vendor contracts, and client agreements. This ensures that the terms are fair, protective, and legally binding.

  3. Mergers and Acquisitions: A & B Law Ventures facilitate mergers, acquisitions, and other business transactions by conducting due diligence, negotiating terms, drafting contracts, and ensuring compliance with regulatory requirements. We also advise on the legal implications and potential risks associated with such transactions.

  4. Corporate Governance: A & B Law Ventures advise businesses on corporate governance matters, including board structure, director and officer duties, shareholder rights, and compliance with corporate regulations. We help companies develop and implement corporate governance policies to promote transparency, accountability, and ethical conduct.

  5. Compliance and Regulatory Matters: A & B Law Ventures assist businesses in navigating complex regulatory frameworks by providing advice on compliance with laws and regulations applicable to their industry. We help clients understand their legal obligations and develop strategies to mitigate compliance risks.

  6. Intellectual Property Protection: We help businesses protect their intellectual property rights by registering trademarks, copyrights, and patents. We also advise on licensing agreements, infringement issues, and enforcement actions to safeguard valuable intellectual assets.

  7. Dispute Resolution: A & B Law Ventures represent clients in resolving disputes through negotiation, mediation, arbitration, or litigation. We handle a wide range of commercial disputes, including breach of contract, business torts, shareholder disputes, and regulatory enforcement actions.

  8. International Business Transactions: With globalization, businesses often engage in cross-border transactions requiring expertise in international law. A & B Law Ventures assist clients in navigating international legal complexities, including foreign investment, trade regulations, and international contracts.

  9. Corporate Restructuring and Insolvency: In times of financial distress, We advise businesses on corporate restructuring, debt restructuring, bankruptcy proceedings, and insolvency matters. They help clients assess their options and develop strategies to reorganize or wind down their operations while minimizing legal and financial risks.

Overall, A & B Law Ventures specializing in corporate and commercial law provide comprehensive legal services tailored to the unique needs and objectives of their clients, helping them navigate the complexities of the business world while minimizing legal risks and maximizing opportunities for growth and success.
