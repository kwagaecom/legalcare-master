Criminal Law

Overview:

A & B Law Ventures Criminal Lawr related services includes:

  • Criminal cases involving offences ofcheating, forgery, violations under Customs Act, Passport Act, Prevention of Corruption Act, Foreign Exchange Management Act (FEMA) and other economic offences
  • Criminal cases pertaining to violations under the Copyright Act, Trade Mark Act, Patent Act, Companies Act, Excise Act, and other Intellectual Property Rights (IPR) laws etc. are also handled by us, along with matters relating to the Negotiable Instruments Act, Bail matters, Criminal Appeals, Criminal Revisions etc

How Can We Help !

A & B Law Ventures specializing in criminal law provide essential assistance to individuals facing criminal charges in various ways:

  1. Legal Advice and Representation: We offer legal advice and representation to individuals accused of committing crimes.We explain the charges, potential penalties, and legal options available to the defendant, guiding them through the complexities of the criminal justice system.

  2. Investigation and Case Analysis: We conduct thorough investigations into the facts surrounding the alleged crime, gathering evidence, interviewing witnesses, and analyzing police reports, forensic reports, and other relevant documentation to build a strong defense strategy.

  3. Courtroom Representation: We represent clients in court proceedings, including arraignments, bail hearings, pretrial motions, plea negotiations, trials, and sentencing hearings. They advocate for their clients' rights and interests at every stage of the criminal proceedings.

  4. Negotiating Plea Bargains: We negotiate plea bargains with prosecutors on behalf of their clients, aiming to secure favorable plea deals that may result in reduced charges, lesser penalties, or alternative sentencing options, such as probation or diversion programs.

  5. Trial Defense: In cases that proceed to trial, We vigorously defend their clients in court, presenting evidence, cross-examining witnesses, and challenging the prosecution's case to obtain acquittals or favorable verdicts.

  6. Appeals and Post-Conviction Relief: A & B Law Ventures handle appeals and post-conviction proceedings for clients who have been convicted of crimes, challenging legal errors, constitutional violations, and miscarriages of justice that may have occurred during the trial or sentencing process.

  7. Alternative Sentencing Options: We advocate for alternative sentencing options for their clients, such as drug treatment programs, mental health counseling, community service, or house arrest, as alternatives to incarceration.

  8. Expungement and Record Sealing: We assist clients in seeking expungement or record sealing of criminal convictions, allowing individuals to move on with their lives without the stigma and collateral consequences associated with a criminal record.

  9. Protection of Constitutional Rights: We protect their clients' constitutional rights, including the right to remain silent, the right to a fair trial, the right to confront witnesses, and the right to effective assistance of counsel, ensuring that law enforcement and prosecutors adhere to legal standards and procedures.

Overall, A & B Law Ventures play a crucial role in safeguarding the rights and interests of individuals accused of crimes, providing skilled advocacy, legal expertise, and personalized representation to achieve the best possible outcomes for their clients.
